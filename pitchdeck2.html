The AI Assistant for Amateur Baseball

Restoring trust, fairness, and development to the game with a powerful, on-device AI umpire assistant.

The Opportunity: Amateur Baseball is in Crisis

The foundation of the game is being undermined by a perfect storm of systemic issues, creating a significant market opportunity for a technology-driven solution.

!

Critical Umpire Shortage

Games are frequently played without qualified officials, leading to inconsistent calls and a diminished experience for players and fans.

Inconsistent Strike Zones

Variable officiating frustrates players and hinders development. Coaches struggle to teach strike zone discipline without objective feedback.

Rising On-Field Hostility

Umpires, often volunteers or young adults, face increasing verbal abuse from coaches and parents, leading to high turnover.

The Solution: The Agentic AI Umpire Assistant

HeyBLU is a smartphone-based AI platform providing the tools to run a smoother, fairer, and more enjoyable game for every stakeholder.

Powerful, Private, and Instantaneous

On-Device & Edge AI

Leveraging powerful neural processors in modern smartphones for zero latency and full offline capability.

Privacy by Design

All audio and video processing happens on the device. No data is sent to the cloud, ensuring total privacy.

"Dashcam" for Incidents

A rolling audio buffer is only saved when an umpire explicitly initiates an incident report, capturing crucial evidence.

A Comprehensive Toolkit for the Modern Game

  • AI-Powered Rulebook: Instant, voice-activated access to league-specific rules (Little League, USSSA, etc.) to resolve disputes quickly.
  • Automated Incident Reporting: A simple, guided process to log on-field incidents with audio evidence for league officials.
  • Ball-Strike Assist: Real-time ball/strike calls delivered discreetly to an earbud, acting as a "co-pilot" to improve accuracy and confidence.
  • Player Development Tools: Heatmaps, pitch tracking, and performance metrics for data-driven coaching feedback.

The Plan: Strategy & Financials

Our phased go-to-market strategy is designed for capital-efficient growth, leading to a clear path to profitability.

Go-To-Market Strategy: A 3-Phase ICP Rollout

1

Empower Umpires

2

Engage Coaches

Connect Parents & Leagues

Phase 1: Empower Umpires (The Wedge)

ICP: Umpire Associations, Tournament Directors, Training Academies.
Strategy: Launch with an "Elite Beta" program, providing the AI Rulebook & Incident Reporting to solve their most acute pains: safety and authority. This builds credibility and gathers crucial testimonials.

Phase 2: Engage Coaches & Teams

ICP: Travel Team Coaches, High School Programs.
Strategy: Introduce advanced player development tools. Drive team adoption by showing clear value in player improvement, and pursue API integrations with platforms like GameChanger to become part of their existing workflow.

Phase 3: Scale to Parents & Leagues

ICP: Youth Leagues (Little League, etc.), Parents.
Strategy: Execute a "National Launch" with parent-focused features (e.g., streaming with strike zone overlay) and league-wide licensing. Drive mass adoption through regional clinics and tournament sponsorships.
